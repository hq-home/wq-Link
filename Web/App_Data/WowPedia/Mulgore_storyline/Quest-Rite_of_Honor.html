Objectives

Kill Chief Squealer Thornmantle.

  •  [Mane of Thornmantle]

Description

Chief Squealer Thornmantle leads these quilboar. It was he that set upon Greatmother Hawkwind and took her life, leading the attack on Camp Narache. He fled and hid afterwards, but we've found him. He lurks to the west within his foul den of thorns, fending off any braves we send in after him.

You've proven yourself as a brave defender of our people, <name>. Kill Thornmantle and bring his mane back to Chief Hawkwind, in honor of our Greatmother!

Rewards

You will be able to choose one of these rewards:
[Slippers of High Honor] [Sunwalker's Belt]
You will also receive: 2
[Bag of Thorns]

Progress

Have you completed your trials, young one?

Completion

<Chief Hawkwind holds the mane of Thornmantle over his head.>

The Chief of the Bristleback is slain. Greatmother Hawkwind's spirit has been avenged!
