Mulgore storyline

From Wowpedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Mulgore is a level 1—10 zone for Horde players.

Red Cloud Mesa

Area map

This is the starting experience for new tauren characters.

  1.  [1] The First Step
  2.  [2] Rite of Strength
  3.  [2] Our Tribe, Imprisoned
  4.  [2] Go to Adana
  5.  [3] Rite of Courage /  [3] Stop the Thorncallers
  6. At level 3, class quests become available:
    •   [3] Verdant Note &   [3] Rejuvenating Touch
    •   [3] Etched Note &   [3] The Hunter's Path
    •   [3] Consecrated Note &   [3] The Way of the Sunwalkers
    •   [3] Hallowed Note &   [3] Healing in a Flash
    •   [3] Rune-Inscribed Note &   [3] Primal Strike
    •   [3] Simple Note &   [3] The First Lesson
    •   [3] Calligraphed Note &   [3] Tiger Palm
  7.  [4] The Battleboars /  [4] Feed of Evil
  8.  [5] Rite of Honor
  9.  [5] Last Rites, First Rites
  10.  [5] Rites of the Earthmother
  11.  [5] Rite of the Winds (to Bloodhoof Village)

Bloodhoof Village

Zone map

This picks up at level 5 in Bloodhoof Village.

  •  [5] Rite of the Winds (from Camp Narache)
  •  [6] Sharing the Land
    •  [7] The Restless Earth
  • Other quests:  [7] Kyle's Gone Missing!,  [6] Swoop Hunting,  [8] Mazzranache,  [8] Dangers of the Windfury
  • Flight path questline:
  1.   [7] A Bundle of Hides
  2.  [7] Ride to Thunder Bluff
  3.  [7] Tal the Wind Rider Master
  4.  [7] Return to Varg
  • Morin Cloudstalker questline:
  1.  [8] Morin Cloudstalker (optional breadcrumb)
  2.  [8] The Ravaged Caravan
  3.  [8] The Ravaged Caravan
  4.  [7] The Venture Co. /  [8] Supervisor Fizsprocket
  • Grimtotem questline (Tauren only):
  1.   [5] Poison Water
  2.   [6] Winterhoof Cleansing
  3.   [7] Thunderhorn Totem
  4.   [8] Thunderhorn Cleansing
  5.   [6] Rite of Vision
  6.   [7] Rite of Vision (to Camp Sungraze)

Camp Sungraze

  •  [10] Preparation for Ceremony
  •  [10] A Sacred Burial
  •  [10] The Hunter's Way
  •  [12] The Demon Scarred Cloak
  •   [10] Rite of Wisdom (after completing   [7] Rite of Vision)
  • Grimtotem questline (Tauren only):
  1.   [7] Rite of Vision (from Bloodhoof Village)
  2.   [10] Wildmane Totem
  3.   [10] Wildmane Cleansing
  4.   [10] Journey into Thunder Bluff
  5.   [10] War Dance
  6.   [12] Walk With The Earth Mother (to Orgrimmar)
